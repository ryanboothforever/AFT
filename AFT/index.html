About Almost FREE Trucks

Almost FREE Trucks was established in 2012 to sell wholesale trucks at great prices. All of our items are sold via eBay auction. We have locations all across the United States including (but not limited to) Georgia, Florida, Pennsylvania, Tennessee, Illinois, Iowa, Texas, and Nevada.

Inventory

Our inventory consists of several different makes and models of sleeper semi tractors, day cabs, and dry van trailers. They come to us as returned leased trucks, retired mail trucks and trailers, accident-damaged units, and trades.
You can find a variety of new and used aftermarket and OEM truck parts at Almost FREE Truck Parts. We add new inventory weekly, so check back often!
