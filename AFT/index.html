About Almost FREE Trucks

Almost FREE Trucks was established in 2012 to sell wholesale trucks at great prices. All of our items are sold via eBay auction. We have locations all across the United States including Georgia, Florida, Pennsylvania, Tennessee, Illinois, Iowa, Texas, and Nevada. We add new inventory weekly, so please check back often!
